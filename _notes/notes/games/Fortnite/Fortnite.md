**Zero Build Mastery Map (4472-5388-8581)** is a Fortnite Creative training map focused on Zero Build fundamentals—movement, aim, positioning, and fighting from natural cover. It’s designed for quick, repeatable drills that mirror real Zero Build combat and works well as a warm-up or skill refresher.
